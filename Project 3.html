Project 3: STFU

This project was specially made for my little sister, as a little part of her graduation present. She told me she was a big fan of the “GOOD evening” painting, which is why I wanted to make one personally for her.
At first, I chose the picture that I wanted to trace, however I did not only want to just trace it I wanted to add my own personal touch to it. Because I am such a big fan of patterns and shapes, I decided to turn my facial features into triangles and fill them out with the the corresponding colour that was in the original picture. I did this because otherwise you would not notice that it was me, nor that it was a face.
Next up, I had to chose a colour for the background. This was easy as I chose her favourite colour, which is pastel rosé.
At last, I added the text “STFU” (shut the f*** up). This is one of our little insiders, as this was something that she said very often to me when I was disturbing her while she was trying to study. For the font I choose Times New Roman, as this was the font that she had to use for her assignments and her exams.
